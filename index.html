Need Programming?

I get asked a lot about what program I'm running. So to start off, I'll go briefly into what we run. Everyone has an opinion on whats the best, and most times it becomes a pretty heated debate on social media, but the thing is, there are virtually thousands upon thousands of programs out there online. You can find any type whether it's linear, undulating or conjugate, or if you're willing to shell out some money for a coach, you can find hundreds of thousands of coaches on Instagram. We personally are running Mike Tuscherer's Reactive training systems in a DUP style program.

For those unfamiliar with Mike Tuscherer, with all the latest and greatest programs that people online are offering these days, you'll notice most are gravitating towards an RPE ( rate of perceived exhaustion) style versus a straight percentage. Who has been credited to bringing the rpe and fatigue scaling to powerlifting programming? You guessed it, it was the man, Mike Tuscherer. If you're familiar with weightlifting programs, you'll notice the Bulgarians have been running rpe training in weightlifting for decades.

Now, we are in no way associated with Mike, but I highly recommend buying his e-book on his site. It goes into depth of the rpe and fatigue scaling as well as how to use his principles.

You can buy it here : ReactiveTrainingSystems.com.
